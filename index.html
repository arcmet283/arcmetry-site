How long does it take to receive my report?
Reports are delivered within 48–72 hours after your completed intake form is submitted. The clock starts when we receive your form — not when you pay. Most clients receive their report within 48 hours.
Is this report specific to my state?
Yes. Every pathway recommendation, salary projection, funding source, and employer reference is specific to your state and metro area. A report for someone in South Carolina will look completely different from one for someone in Ohio or Texas.
What if I have a barrier like a criminal record or no car?
Barriers are not filtered out of your report — they are built into it. A criminal record, transportation dependency, or childcare constraint will directly shape which pathways are recommended and how the action plan is structured.
Do I need a resume to get started?
No. The intake form captures your work history, skills, and goals through 15 straightforward questions. A resume is optional and helps with more specific guidance, but it is not required.
What format is the report delivered in?
Reports are delivered as a professionally formatted PDF to your email inbox. No login required, no account to manage — just your report in your inbox, ready to read or print.
